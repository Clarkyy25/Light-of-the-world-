Personal Reflection

In our digital age, it's important to maintain our spiritual values online. Being a light in the digital world means sharing positivity, truth, and kindness in all our both online interactions and personal.

"You are the light of the world. Atown built on a hill cannot be hidden." - Matthew 5:14

This reminds us that our digital presence should reflect our faith and values, shining God's light in online spaces.

Educational Values

To be a student is to gather light.To be the 'light of the world' is to know why we gather it. My education at Tangub City Global College is the process of building this light—not to hide it, but to let it shine through my actions, my knowledge, and my values, illuminating darkness and guiding the way for others.

Inspirational Value

Carry your light with courage, for this world needs your unique spark. Do not be afraid of the darkness, for it is only a canvas upon which your brightness can shine even more brilliantly. Shine not just for yourself, but for all those who are still finding their own way. Your radiance has the power to illuminate paths and warm hearts. So go forth, and be the light.
